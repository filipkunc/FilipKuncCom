Click anywhere to move the goal and watch a few thousand agents reroute instantly. Draw some
walls in their way. Every agent reads its direction from a shared flow field, and no agent owns
a path.

<FlowField />

The simulation runs in Rust compiled to WebAssembly, and the renderer is instanced WebGL2. The
field structure follows the Supreme Commander 2 architecture from Elijah Emerson's Game AI Pro
chapter: an 8-bit cost field where 255 means wall, an integration field holding the integrated
cost plus flags, and an 8-bit flow field packing a direction index and per-cell flags.

The integration view above shows the integrated cost as a gradient with the line-of-sight
region brightened. Switch to the flow view to see the direction every cell hands to agents
standing in it.
